Deposits
We currently accept USDT, BTC, ETH, SOL, LTC, and TON. Each coin supports multiple networks — for example USDT is available on TRC20, ERC20, and BEP20. Always confirm the correct network before sending to avoid loss of funds.
Once you submit your deposit request, it reflects typically within 1 to 30 minutes. You will see the status update from pending to approved in your Deposit History, and your wallet balance will be credited instantly on approval.
The minimum deposit is $5 USD equivalent in any supported cryptocurrency. There is no maximum deposit limit.
Unfortunately, blockchain transactions are irreversible. Funds sent to the wrong address or on the wrong network cannot be recovered. Always double-check the wallet address and selected network before sending. Contact our support on WhatsApp immediately if you believe there is an issue.
💸
Withdrawals
Go to Withdraw in the sidebar or menubar. Enter the amount you want to withdraw and your Litecoin (LTC) wallet address. Submit the request. LTC will be sent to your address within 1 - 30 minutes. All withdrawals are paid exclusively in LTC.
The Free plan is a starter plan gifted to every new member worth $5. Withdrawals are disabled on this plan to encourage active participation. To unlock withdrawals, upgrade to the Basic ($20), Executive ($50), Premium ($100), Elite ($500), or Prestige ($1000) plan using your earned balance or a fresh deposit.
The minimum withdrawal is $5.00 USD equivalent in LTC. Withdrawal requests below this amount will not be processed.
Withdrawals are processed within 1 - 30 minutes. If yours has been pending longer than 30 minutes, please contact support with your username and withdrawal amount and we'll resolve it promptly.

Mining Plans
We offer 6 plans — all run for 30 days:

🌱 Free — $5 (gifted on signup) · 100% ROI · No withdrawals
⚡ Basic — $20 · 150% ROI · Withdrawals enabled
🏆 Executive — $50 · 175% ROI · Withdrawals enabled
💎 Premium — $100 · 200% ROI · Withdrawals enabled
🔷 Elite — $500 · Custom ROI · Withdrawals enabled
👑 Prestige — $1000 · Custom ROI · Withdrawals enabled
Yes. Activating a new plan will replace your current active plan immediately. The remaining days of your old plan will not be refunded, so we recommend activating a new plan only after your current plan expires or when upgrading intentionally.
Once your 30-day plan expires, AI stops trading and mining stops automatically and your balance retains all earned rewards. You can then activate a new plan using your available wallet balance or make a fresh deposit to continue earning.

Rewards & Referrals
Rewards are distributed automatically every 24 hours from when you activated your plan. They are credited directly to your wallet balance. You can see the countdown to your next reward on your Dashboard under the Mining widget.
Share your unique referral code with anyone. When they register using your code and activate a mining plan, you earn 10% of their activated plan — automatically. For example, if they activate the $100 Premium plan, you get $10 credited to your wallet instantly. You can track all your referrals and commissions on the Referral page in the sidebar.
Team Leader bonuses are one-time cash rewards for growing your referral network:

👥 $50 bonus — when you reach 20 active mining members in your team
🌟 $100 bonus — when you scale to 50 active mining members

These are credited automatically to your wallet the moment a member's activation triggers the threshold. You can track your progress on the Referral page.
Your referral code and shareable link are visible on the Referral page in the sidebar. You can copy the code or link directly, or use the share buttons to send it to WhatsApp, Telegram, or Twitter in one tap.
