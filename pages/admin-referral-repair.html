Checking admin access…

Step 1 — Load All Users

Fetches every user account. Use this to find the referrer's UID and the referred user's UID for the steps below.

Step 2 — Assign Referral (set referredBy)

For users who registered before the fix was deployed, their referredBy field is null. Paste their UID and the referrer's UID below to link them correctly. You can assign multiple users to the same referrer one at a time.

Step 3 — Credit Missed Referral Commission

For users who activated a paid plan before referral-bonus.js was deployed, no commission was recorded. Enter the referrer's UID, the plan they activated, and click Credit. This pays 10% of the plan cost to the referrer's wallet instantly.

Step 4 — Bulk Auto-Assign via Referral Codes

Scans every user who has a referral code in referralCodes collection but whose referredBy in the users collection is empty. This can only fix users who entered a valid referral code during registration but whose link wasn't saved due to the bug. Run this first — it may resolve most cases automatically.
